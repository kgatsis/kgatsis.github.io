I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) at the Department of Engineering Science in the University of Oxford, UK. I am a member of the Control Engineering Group.

I work on control, learning, optimization, security, and privacy for autonomous applications in the Internet-of-Things (IoT). You will find more information on my research page and my publications page.

Feel free to contact me if you are interested in pursuing a doctoral program at Oxford under my supervision.

Before joining Oxford, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Electrical and Systems Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania, where I also obtained my PhD degree (Dec. 2016). I worked in the GRASP lab under the supervision of Professor George J. Pappas.
